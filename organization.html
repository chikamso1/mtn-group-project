Management Team

Meet the leadership driving MTN Nigeria's success

Karl Olutokun Toriola

Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

Karl leads MTN Nigeria with over 24 years of telecommunications experience. He holds a BSc in Electronic and Electrical Engineering from Obafemi Awolowo University and an MSc in Communication Systems from University of Wales, Swansea.

Modupe Kadri

Chief Financial Officer (CFO)

Modupe is a Chartered Accountant with over 30 years of cross-functional business and finance experience. He oversees MTN Nigeria's financial operations and strategic financial planning.

Ayham Moussa

Chief Technology Officer (CTO)

Ayham leads MTN Nigeria's technology strategy and network operations, ensuring optimal service delivery and technological advancement across our infrastructure.

Onyinye Ikenna Emeka

Chief Marketing Officer (CMO)

Onyinye drives MTN Nigeria's marketing strategy, brand management, and customer experience initiatives, ensuring strong market positioning and customer satisfaction.

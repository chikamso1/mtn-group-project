About Us

Our History

MTN Nigeria was launched in 2001 as part of the MTN Group, which was originally founded in South Africa in 1994 as M-Cell. MTN Nigeria began operations on August 8, 2001, and made its first call at Maritime House, Apapa, Lagos, marking the beginning of a telecommunications revolution in Nigeria.

As a proudly Nigerian company with a rich African heritage, MTN Nigeria has grown to become the largest mobile network operator in Nigeria and one of the most significant telecommunications companies in Africa. Over two decades, we have consistently invested in advanced communication infrastructure and innovative services.

From our humble beginnings with limited network coverage, we have expanded to serve over 77 million subscribers across all 36 states and the Federal Capital Territory. Our journey has been marked by numerous milestones including the launch of 2G, 3G, 4G, and most recently, 5G services in Nigeria.
